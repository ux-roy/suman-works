Profile Depiction

I love to paint on canvas. As a UX professional, I am passionate about crafting easy-to-use solutions and enjoyable experiences for enterprise platforms, consumer products, and digital services. I focus on understanding real-time user needs through research, feedback, and testing.I connect the dots between human behavior patterns and computing algorithms using the golden ratio of design principles.I believe design has the power to unite a diverse mindset.

After schooling, I pursued a Bachelor of Science degree from the esteemed Maharaja Bir Bikram College. However, the call for art was too strong to resist. To follow my passion, I enrolled at the Govt. College of Art and Crafts to study painting and graduated with a bachelor's degree in fine arts. During this time, I also gained practical experience by working as an art teacher for children and participating in Durga Puja festive decorations.

With dreams in my eyes and determination in my heart, I moved to Bangalore. The initial phase was fraught with challenges, but they only served to strengthen me. As I navigated through these trials, my preferences evolved, leading me to embark on a new journey in design. As a design professional, I am at the crossroads of science and art. It’s like being a magician, using the wand of passion to turn the impossible into possible.

Design Expertise

Design Thinking

The creative ideation process challenges usual assumptions rather than identifying alternative techniques.

Cognitive Empathy

Ability to understand another person perspective. It involves being able to put yourself in someone else shoes.

Human Ergonomics

The interactions between humans and other elements of a system. It’s all about making products simple, clear, and easy to use.

Ethnographic Research

A qualitative method of direct observation in order to capture behavioural activity in their natural environment.

Persona Development

A user persona is a fictional character that represents a specific type of audience for any product or service.

Information Architecture

The disciplined way of organizing, labelling & presenting information in a way that makes it easy to find & use.

Data Visualization

Graphical representation of data using visual elements like charts, plots, maps, infographics & even animations.

Wireframe & Prototypes

A skeletal layout that explains elements & features of concept & a dynamic replica of that same to improve accuracy.

Interaction Design

The creation of physical or emotional dialogue between humans & digital environments or interactive systems.

Usability Testing

Crafted Iconography
