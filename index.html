Profile Depiction

I love to paint on canvas. As a UX professional, I am passionate about crafting easy-to-use solutions and enjoyable experiences for enterprise platforms, consumer products, and digital services. I focus on understanding real-time user needs through research, feedback, and testing.I connect the dots between human behavior patterns and computing algorithms using the golden ratio of design principles.I believe design has the power to unite a diverse mindset.

After schooling, I pursued a Bachelor of Science degree from the esteemed Maharaja Bir Bikram College. However, the call for art was too strong to resist. To follow my passion, I enrolled at the Govt. College of Art and Crafts to study painting and graduated with a bachelor's degree in fine arts. During this time, I also gained practical experience by working as an art teacher for children and participating in Durga Puja festive decorations.

With dreams in my eyes and determination in my heart, I moved to Bangalore. The initial phase was fraught with challenges, but they only served to strengthen me. As I navigated through these trials, my preferences evolved, leading me to embark on a new journey in design. As a design professional, I am at the crossroads of science and art.
