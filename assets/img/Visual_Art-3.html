Title: Couple's POV

Time:
2010

Medium:
Mix-Media on plywood

Depiction:
Two figures bend toward each other like reflections trying to meet. Their bodies are simple, but their emotions feel complex — the kind of closeness where love, doubt, comfort, and unspoken words all mix together. The monochrome palette strips everything down to pure feeling. This artwork captures the delicate dance of relationships: trying to understand, trying to be understood, and learning to stay connected even when it’s hard.
