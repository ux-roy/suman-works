Title: My Low Tide

Time:
2015

Medium:
Acrylic on canvas

Depiction:
A golden figure curls inward, resting in a moment of emotional low tide. The background feels textured and heavy, echoing that quiet ache we all experience during difficult phases. Yet the warm color of the figure suggests something important — this is not the end of the journey. It’s a pause, a breath, a gathering of strength. The piece gently reminds us that lows are temporary, and even in our quietest moments, a small light continues to glow within.
