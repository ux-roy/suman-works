Title: The Word Father

Time:
2018

Medium:
Oil on canvas

Depiction:
A weathered face rises from a shadowed background, shaped with strong strokes that feel almost carved. The expression holds a mix of firmness and sadness, like someone who has carried responsibilities for many years. You can sense stories behind those eyes — struggles endured, wisdom earned, love shown in ways that weren’t always spoken aloud. This painting becomes a tribute to the silent pillars in our lives, the ones who stand strong even when life feels heavy.
