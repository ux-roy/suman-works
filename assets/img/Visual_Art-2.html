Title: The Golden Cage

Time:
2008

Medium:
Acrylic on canvas

Depiction:
A beautiful path opens up under soft sunlight, framed by tall trees and distant houses. It feels inviting, almost dreamy — but the bright gate at the center blocks the way forward. This contrast creates an emotional pull: beauty that feels close, yet unreachable. The scene reflects how life sometimes shows us wonderful things, but timing or circumstances ask us to hold back. It’s a painting about longing, limits, and the quiet hope that gates eventually open.
