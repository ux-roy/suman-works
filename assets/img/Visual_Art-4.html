Title: Me Time Thinker

Time:
2015

Medium:
Acrylic on canvas

Depiction:
A lone figure glows softly in a swirl of darker tones, as if their thoughts are lighting them from within. They sit in a moment that feels deeply personal — the kind of inner conversation we only have with ourselves. This piece celebrates the quiet strength that comes from pausing, reflecting, and listening to your own heart. It’s a reminder that solitude isn’t emptiness; it’s a space where healing and clarity begin.
